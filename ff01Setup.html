Few things to do before embarking on your journey..

1. Click roll initial stats until you're happy with the numbers (Hint: the higher the better)

2. Click starting potion

3. Click Start adventure when done

Initial Skill: Roll 1 die +6

Initial Stamina: Roll 2 die +12

Initial Luck: Roll 1 die +6


Choose 1 of the 3 starting potions, each potion has 2 doses and can be taken anytime

Potion of Skill - restores skill points to initial value

Potion of Strength - restores stamina points to initial value

Potion of Fortune - increase initial luck score by 1 and restores luck to new inital value
